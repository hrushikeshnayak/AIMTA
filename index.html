About AIMTA 2025

Background

Advances and Innovation in Metaverse Technologies & Applications (AIMTA 2025) conference brings together leading researchers, practitioners, and industry professionals from around the globe to share and discuss recent advancements and innovations in metaverse technologies and their applications across various domains, including technology, management, education, and business.

This convergence of digital transformation and intelligent systems is crucial for shaping the future of virtual interactions, decentralized economies, and immersive experiences. By focusing on emerging trends and interdisciplinary applications, the conference seeks to highlight cutting-edge technologies and methodologies that will drive innovation, enhance connectivity, and redefine the digital landscape.

Conference Theme

Advancements and Applications of Metaverse Technologies in Engineering, Management, and Sustainable Digital Transformation.

About Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth

Established in 2000 under the leadership of visionary philanthropist Dr. D. Y. Patil Sir, Dr. D. Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pune (DPU) is a private, non-profit, self-financing organization dedicated to serving society and making a difference.

Starting with the Dr. D. Y. Patil Medical College & Research Centre, the university has grown to encompass 14 professional institutes in fields including Medicine, Dentistry, Nursing, Physiotherapy, Optometry, Biotechnology, Homoeopathy, Ayurveda, Management, Design, Allied Health Sciences, Science and Technology, and Liberal Arts.

The institutions have earned prominence by providing quality education to more than 4000 students across India and the world, nurturing professionals in various fields including healthcare, biotechnology, and management.
